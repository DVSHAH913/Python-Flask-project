Let's learn some quick tips about technical Ananlysis:


Exponential Moving Average(EMA):

7-day EMA on low price(LH):


If close price is greater then 7LH:
Buy and your target price is 7 EH

Else if close price is less than 7LH:
Sell and your target price is 7EH

Else:
Wait and watch

7-day EMA on high price(EH):

If close price greater than 7EH:
Buy and your target price is 7LH

Else if close price is less than 7EH
Sell and your target price is 7LH

Else:
Wait and watch

Divisionals:

Low divisional(sum of 7-day EMA, 14-day EMA and 21-day EMA on low prices):

If close price greater than Low divisional:
Buy and your target price is High divisional

Else if close price is less than Low divisional:
Sell and your target price is High divisional

Else:
Wait and watch

High divisional(sum of 7-day EMA, 14-day EMA and 21-day EMA on high prices):


If close price greater than High divisional:
Buy and your target price is Low divisional

Else if close price less than High divisional:
Sell and your target price is Low divisional

Else:
Wait and watch

Bollinger Band:

If close price is greater than Bollinger middle:
Buy

Else if close price is less than Bollinger middle :
Sell

Else:
Wait and watch

Relative Strength Index(RSI):

If RSI is greater than 30:
Then bullish signal which means buy

Else if RSI is less than 30:
Then bearish signal which means sell

Else:
Wait and Watch

Stochastic Oscillator:

If stochastic greater than 5:
Then bullish signal which means buy

Else if stochastic is less than 5:
Then bearish signal which means sell

Else:
Wait and Watch

Moving Average Convergence Divergence(MACD):

If MACD is greater than 30:
Then bullish signal which means buy

Else if MACD is less than 30:
Then bearish signal which means sell

Else:
Wait and watch.
